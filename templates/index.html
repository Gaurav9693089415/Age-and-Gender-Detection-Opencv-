About FaceAging Detection

FaceAging Detection is an advanced AI-powered web application that accurately predicts the age and gender of individuals from uploaded images. Utilizing state-of-the-art machine learning models, it provides quick and reliable results to help users gain insights into facial characteristics.

Our Services

  • Age Prediction: Accurately estimate the age of a person from their facial image.
  • Gender Detection: Identify the gender with high confidence using AI models.
  • Fast & Secure: Upload images securely with quick processing times.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for all users.
